About This Project

This interactive timeline celebrates the 11th anniversary of MyGov, the pioneering platform that has transformed citizen engagement in India. It showcases the journey of how a simple idea grew into the world's largest platform for participatory governance, highlighting key achievements and its profound impact on the nation.

The project aims to demonstrate the importance of such platforms in a democracy, chronicling the successful events and initiatives that have empowered millions of Indian citizens to become partners in nation-building.
